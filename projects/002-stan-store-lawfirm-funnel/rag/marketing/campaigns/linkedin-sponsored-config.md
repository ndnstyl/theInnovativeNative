## Optimization Strategy

### Week 1: Testing
- Run all 5 ad variants
- Test both audience segments
- Monitor CTR and engagement
- Identify top 2 performers

### Week 2: Optimization
- Pause underperforming ads (<0.3% CTR)
- Increase budget on winners
- Test headline variations
- Consider carousel format

### Week 3-4: Conversion Push
- Focus budget on highest converting ads
- Add urgency messaging ("X slots left")
- Retarget website visitors (if budget allows)
- A/B test landing page elements
