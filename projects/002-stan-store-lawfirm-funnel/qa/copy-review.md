## Executive Summary

This review assesses all copy deliverables for the Stan Store Law Firm Funnel against the following criteria:
1. **Legibility** (scannability, paragraph density, visual flow)
2. **Brand Alignment** (direct, practical, anti-sleaze, "builders not experts")
3. **Compelling Quality** (would a $1M managing partner keep reading?)
4. **Consistency** (numbers, CTAs, positioning alignment)
5. **Legal Compliance** (attorney advertising ethics, claim substantiation)
6. **Completeness** (placeholders, broken references)

**Overall Assessment:** Strong foundation with several critical gaps and compliance risks that MUST be addressed before launch.

### Legal Compliance Issues

**CRITICAL COMPLIANCE GAP (Line 414):**

This disclaimer is at the VERY END of a 414-line document. If someone takes the scorecard and makes a business decision that goes poorly, this disclaimer may not provide adequate protection. Recommendation: Add a shorter version near the top (after the intro, before Section 1).

**MODERATE RISK (Line 170-172):**
"I was talking with a criminal defense attorney last year who realized he was losing $80K annually just from unbilled time..."

Is this a real case study or a composite? If real, do you have permission to reference it (even anonymized)? If composite, should be disclosed. ## LEGAL COMPLIANCE: JURISDICTIONAL RISK ASSESSMENT

### Attorney Advertising Rules (ABA Model Rules 7.1-7.3)

**RULE 7.1 - Communications Concerning a Lawyer's Services**
- "A lawyer shall not make a false or misleading communication about the lawyer or the lawyer's services."

**POTENTIAL VIOLATIONS FLAGGED:**

1. **Unsubstantiated Claims:**
   - "15-30% of billable time lost" (blueprint Line 34) - Cites ABA research but no specific source
   - "21 times more likely to convert" (blueprint Line 44) - Cites general sales research, not law-specific
   - "$180K malpractice claim" (content calendar Post 4) - No substantiation provided

   **FIX:** Add footnotes with actual citations OR reframe as "industry research suggests" instead of definitive claims.

2. **Testimonials Without Disclaimer:**
   - Scorecard Line 170: "I was talking with a criminal defense attorney..." - No disclaimer
   - Content calendar Post 12: Testimonial from "Managing Partner, Criminal Defense Firm" - No disclaimer

   **FIX:** Add disclaimer: "Results described are from a specific client engagement and may not be representative of all clients. No guarantee of similar results is made."

3. **Projections Presented as Certainty:**
   - Scorecard Line 151: "That is $126,000 in additional annual revenue" (not "potential" or "estimated")
   - Blueprint Line 444: ROI table shows specific dollar amounts without qualification

   **FIX:** Add "estimated" or "potential" throughout. Ensure final disclaimer explicitly addresses financial projections.

**RULE 7.2 - Advertising**
- Some states require "ATTORNEY ADVERTISING" disclaimer on all marketing materials. Your documents include this in final disclaimers but not prominently.

**FIX:** Check if Stan Store landing pages require "ATTORNEY ADVERTISING" header per target states' rules.

**RULE 7.3 - Solicitation of Clients**
- Prohibits real-time solicitation (phone, in-person) but generally allows written marketing.
- Your funnel is compliant (all opt-in).

**RECOMMENDATION:** Before launch, run ALL copy past a legal marketing compliance specialist in your target states (particularly CA, NY, FL, TX which have strict advertising rules).
