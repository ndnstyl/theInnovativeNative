\---

\#\# 🌟 1\. Key Light  
\*\*Description:\*\* Primary and most important light in a lighting setup.    
\*\*Use:\*\* Shapes the subject’s form and separates them from background; basis of other lighting.    
\*\*Effect:\*\* Defines primary exposure and contrast in a scene. \[oai\_citation:14‡Wikipedia\](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Key\_light?utm\_source=chatgpt.com)

\---

\#\# 🌗 2\. Rembrandt Lighting  
\*\*Description:\*\* Distinct triangle of light on the shadow side of the face.    
\*\*Use:\*\* Dramatic, classic cinematic portrait style.    
\*\*Effect:\*\* Blend of light and shadow with painterly depth. \[oai\_citation:15‡Wikipedia\](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rembrandt\_lighting?utm\_source=chatgpt.com)

\---

\#\# 🦋 3\. Butterfly Lighting  
\*\*Description:\*\* Light positioned above and in front of subject creating butterfly-shaped shadow under nose.    
\*\*Use:\*\* Glamorous, flattering look popular in fashion and portraiture.    
\*\*Effect:\*\* Soft yet structured shadows; lifts cheekbones and jawline. \[oai\_citation:16‡ppa.com\](https://www.ppa.com/ppmag/articles/9-types-of-portrait-lighting?utm\_source=chatgpt.com)

\---

\#\# 👤 4\. Loop Lighting  
\*\*Description:\*\* Light placed at a slight angle to cast a small shadow from the nose.    
\*\*Use:\*\* Balance between flattering light and mild dimensionality.    
\*\*Effect:\*\* Gentle depth, standard cinematic portraits. \[oai\_citation:17‡ppa.com\](https://www.ppa.com/ppmag/articles/9-types-of-portrait-lighting?utm\_source=chatgpt.com)

\---

\#\# ⚔️ 5\. Split Lighting  
\*\*Description:\*\* Light source at 90º so one side of face is illuminated and other in shadow.    
\*\*Use:\*\* High drama, high contrast, psychological intensity.    
\*\*Effect:\*\* Sharp division of tone and mood. \[oai\_citation:18‡Digital Photo Mentor\](https://www.digitalphotomentor.com/lessons/portrait-lighting-lighting-patterns/?utm\_source=chatgpt.com)

\---

\#\# 🌑 6\. Short Lighting  
\*\*Description:\*\* Light illuminates the side of the face turned away from camera.    
\*\*Use:\*\* Slimming affect, more shadow on near side.    
\*\*Effect:\*\* Depth, contrast, contour emphasis. \[oai\_citation:19‡ppa.com\](https://www.ppa.com/ppmag/articles/9-types-of-portrait-lighting?utm\_source=chatgpt.com)

\---

\#\# ☀️ 7\. Broad Lighting  
\*\*Description:\*\* Light illuminates the side of the face closest to camera.    
\*\*Use:\*\* Wider, softer, more even lighting.    
\*\*Effect:\*\* Less contrast; suitable for classic or gentle scenes. \[oai\_citation:20‡ppa.com\](https://www.ppa.com/ppmag/articles/9-types-of-portrait-lighting?utm\_source=chatgpt.com)

\---

\#\# ✨ 8\. Backlight / Rim Lighting  
\*\*Description:\*\* Light placed behind subject, creating outline highlights.    
\*\*Use:\*\* Separates subject from background, adds dimension.    
\*\*Effect:\*\* Highlights edges; can produce glowing silhouette. \[oai\_citation:21‡ppa.com\](https://www.ppa.com/ppmag/articles/9-types-of-portrait-lighting?utm\_source=chatgpt.com)

\---

\#\# 🐚 9\. Clamshell Lighting  
\*\*Description:\*\* Butterfly lighting plus reflector or fill below.    
\*\*Use:\*\* Smooth, flattering light on subject.    
\*\*Effect:\*\* Soft shadows, even tone on face. \[oai\_citation:22‡ppa.com\](https://www.ppa.com/ppmag/articles/9-types-of-portrait-lighting?utm\_source=chatgpt.com)
