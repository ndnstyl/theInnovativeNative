# **📽️ Visual Composition AI Agents — Cinematic Commercial Ad**

One AI Agent per core visual function — each responsible for a specific aspect of visual storytelling. This list omits general production and technical execution to focus purely on *crafted imagery*.  
---

## **🎨 1\.** 

## **Mood Board & Aesthetic Direction AI**

**Purpose:** Establishes visual tone and emotional feeling

**Core Responsibilities**

* Creates concept boards representing *lighting style, color palette, texture, atmosphere,* and *emotional tone*

* Synthesizes references from brand materials and cinematic inspirations

* Defines *visual language guidelines* that other agents follow

* Outputs cohesive visual mood boards and style inspiration comps

**Deliverables**

* Mood board images

* Style guide (color palettes, reference frames)

* Visual adjectives and thematic statements

---

## **📘 2\.** 

## **Shot List & Story Flow AI**

**Purpose:** Plans camera compositions and narrative visuals

**Core Responsibilities**

* Translates script/outline into visual sequences

* Defines individual shots: *purpose, framing, camera movement, duration,* and *transitions*

* Organizes visual flow into sequences or beats with linked visual references

* Ensures composition choices support *emotion and brand message*

**Deliverables**

* Ordered Shot List (scene → shot → description)

* Visual framing references

* Movement annotations (e.g., slow push-in, wide establishing, close emotion)

---

## **📐 3\.** 

## **Production Design & Set Visuals AI**

**Purpose:** Designs the visual *environment* and spatial look

**Core Responsibilities**

* Defines what the physical or virtual space *feels like*: textures, materials, props, spatial energy

* Aligns environment color, shapes, and surface qualities to mood board

* Generates room/scene layout concepts and visual references

* Ensures environment *supports the narrative without stealing focus*

**Deliverables**

* Set design concepts

* Color and material schematics

* Layout boards

---

## **👗 4\.** 

## **Wardrobe & Character Style AI**

**Purpose:** Crafts *visual character identity* through clothing and styling

**Core Responsibilities**

* Designs wardrobes that *complement mood boards and set visuals*

* Chooses color palettes, fabrics, silhouettes that work with lighting and visual tone

* Ensures outfit continuity across scenes

* Generates lookbooks and outfit guides per character

**Deliverables**

* Wardrobe lookbooks \+ reference images

* Color & texture style sheets

* Consistency guidelines per scene

---

## **🎞️ 5\.** 

## **Style Synthesis Director AI**

**Purpose:** Ensures **cohesion** across all visual elements

**Core Responsibilities**

* Reviews outputs from Mood Board, Shot List, Production Design, and Wardrobe AI

* Resolves visual conflicts (color clashes, mood inconsistencies, framing mismatch)

* Harmonizes visual language into a single *cohesive aesthetic brief*

* Prepares final visual style bible for the shoot or rendering pipeline

**Deliverables**

* Final Visual Style Bible

* Cross-department alignment notes

* Unified visual directive
