# BOWTIE BULLIES -- THE AFTERMATH
# Brand Alignment Matrix

**Purpose:** Universal scoring system for every agent, workflow, and collaborator producing content for BowTie Bullies. Any deliverable -- script, thumbnail, visual, VO, community post, affiliate integration, or automation output -- can be evaluated against this matrix to determine whether it is on-brand, off-brand, or in the gray zone.

**Date:** 2026-02-10
**Source:** Synthesized from 7 domain audits (Narrative, Visual, YouTube, Video Production, Brand Identity, Marketing, Automation)

---

## HOW TO USE THIS MATRIX

1. **Before producing:** Check the relevant domain columns for guardrails
2. **During production:** Score your output against each applicable dimension
3. **After production:** Run the Quick Scorecard (Section 5) -- anything below 70% aggregate needs revision
4. **For QA gates:** The Pre-Publish Checklist (Section 6) is the minimum bar

---

## 1. SCORING SCALE

| Score | Label | Meaning | Action |
|-------|-------|---------|--------|
| **5** | Core Brand | Perfectly embodies the brand thesis | Ship it |
| **4** | On-Brand | Aligns with brand identity, minor polish only | Ship with minor notes |
| **3** | Neutral | Doesn't violate brand, but doesn't embody it either | Revise -- add brand DNA |
| **2** | Off-Brand | Contradicts one or more brand pillars | Do not ship -- rework |
| **1** | Brand Violation | Actively damages the brand identity | Reject and flag |

**Minimum shipping threshold:** Average score of 3.5 across all applicable dimensions, with NO dimension scoring below 2.

## 2. BRAND ALIGNMENT DIMENSIONS

### Dimension 1: VOICE (Tyrone)

| Score | What It Sounds Like |
|-------|-------------------|
| **5** | Short, measured sentences. Subtext over confession. Silence as emphasis. Feels earned. "The algorithm doesn't know your name. But it knows your zip code." |
| **4** | Correct tone but slightly over-explains or adds one extra sentence. Still sounds like Tyrone but could be tighter. |
| **3** | Generic narration that could be anyone. Not wrong, but not Tyrone. No distinctive rhythm. |
| **2** | Uses buzzwords, therapy talk, hype language, or YouTuber energy. "Hey guys, today we're going to explore..." |
| **1** | Motivational yelling, chest-beating, trauma dumping, over-sharing, or corporate speak. "Leverage your potential on this healing journey!" |

**Quick test:** Read it aloud at 115 WPM. If it feels like you're rushing, it's not Tyrone. If the pauses feel heavy, it is.

**Voice Red Lines (instant score of 1):**
- "Hey guys / hey everyone" opening
- Exclamation marks in scripts
- "Smash that subscribe button"
- Therapy/self-help language ("healing journey," "manifest," "level up")
- Corporate jargon ("leverage," "synergy," "paradigm shift")
- YouTuber hype ("INSANE," "MIND-BLOWING," "you won't BELIEVE")

### Dimension 2: VISUAL IDENTITY (Red Nose + Aesthetic)

| Score | What It Looks Like |
|-------|-------------------|
| **5** | Truth Tones palette. Dark Steel base. Rust Orange accent. Red Nose present with correct markers (scar, bowtie, amber eyes, red/liver nose). Film grain. Vignette. Cinematic. "You can feel the concrete." |
| **4** | Correct palette and Red Nose, but framing, lighting, or composition is slightly off. Dog is correct but environment doesn't quite match. |
| **3** | Generic dark aesthetic that doesn't violate the palette but lacks the urban texture, the warmth of Rust Orange, or the specific grit. Could be any dark brand. |
| **2** | Bright colors, neon, clean/modern aesthetic, wrong dog breed or markings, bowtie missing, wrong color accent. |
| **1** | Human faces visible. Cartoon/anime style. Corporate stock photos. Saturated colors. Red Nose aggressive (baring teeth, lunging). |

**Visual Red Lines (instant score of 1):**
- Human faces in frame
- Neon or saturated colors
- Cartoon/illustration/anime style
- Red Nose baring teeth, aggressive posture, or in a fight
- Bright, sunny, cheerful imagery
- Corporate or suburban environments
- Text using fonts other than Bangers / Montserrat / Anton
- The color Amber (#D4A03C) appearing anywhere

**Palette Reference (canonical):**

### Dimension 3: RED NOSE CHARACTER CONSISTENCY

| Score | What It Looks Like |
|-------|-------------------|
| **5** | Exact breed markers: fawn/tawny coat, red/liver nose, amber/whiskey eyes, natural uncropped ears, left-cheek scar, dark red silk bowtie at 2 o'clock. Composed expression. Mouth closed. No collar. |
| **4** | Mostly correct -- minor deviation on one marker (slightly wrong ear shape, scar not visible but not contradicted). Still recognizable as Red Nose. |
| **3** | Generic pitbull that isn't wrong but lacks the specific markers. No scar, generic brown eyes, bowtie color off. |
| **2** | Wrong breed characteristics (blue nose, cropped ears, brindle coat). Bowtie missing. Aggressive or playful expression. |
| **1** | Different breed entirely. Cartoon dog. Red Nose in a collar. Red Nose with a human. Red Nose running, jumping, or playing. |

**Character Red Lines (instant score of 1):**
- Wrong breed (any non-American Pitbull Terrier)
- Cropped ears (must be natural/uncropped)
- Blue/black/gray nose (must be red/liver)
- Collar of any kind (bowtie ONLY)
- Aggressive expression (teeth showing, lunging, snarling)
- Playful behavior (tail wagging excessively, play bow, rolling over)
- Human figures in the same frame (silhouettes acceptable per future governance)

### Dimension 4: NARRATIVE STRUCTURE

| Score | What It Looks Like |
|-------|-------------------|
| **5** | Follows the 6-section template (Hook, Context, List, Deeper Layer, The Move, Close). Emotional arc builds. "The Deeper Layer" connects present to historical pattern. Closes with weight, not hype. |
| **4** | Follows structure but one section is weak (e.g., "The Move" is generic, or hook uses an overused pattern). Overall arc is present. |
| **3** | Delivers information but lacks narrative arc. Feels like a list, not a story. No "turn" moment. No tension/release. |
| **2** | Random structure. No clear hook. No deeper layer. Ends without resolution or landing. Feels like a blog post read aloud. |
| **1** | Intro starts with "Hey guys." No hook. Pure information dump. Asks for subscribers before delivering value. Closes with hype. |

**Narrative Red Lines (instant score of 1):**
- Opening with a greeting
- No hook in first 30 seconds
- Asking to subscribe before delivering value
- No "deeper layer" connecting AI to systemic patterns
- Closing with "like and subscribe" energy

### Dimension 6: AFFILIATE/PRODUCT INTEGRATION

| Score | What It Looks/Sounds Like |
|-------|--------------------------|
| **5** | Natural, brief, Tyrone-voice. "I keep one of these in my bag. $12. Link below." Product mentioned once, organically, within content flow. Red Nose beside product (P3 pose). FTC-compliant disclosure in description. |
| **4** | Product mention is on-brand but slightly forced or repeated one extra time. Still brief. Still Tyrone's voice. Disclosure present. |

| **2** | Hard sell. Multiple mentions. "Go buy this NOW." Infomercial energy. Discount codes shouted. Disclosure missing or insufficient. |
| **1** | "Use my code TYRONE15 for 15% off!" Full sponsor read in non-Tyrone voice. No FTC disclosure. Product unrelated to content. |

**Affiliate Red Lines (instant score of 1):**
- No FTC-compliant disclosure in description (above the fold)
- Discount code energy ("Use code X for Y% off!")
- Product mention longer than 15 seconds in video
- Product unrelated to episode content
- Hard sell language ("You NEED this")
- Same product pitch copy-pasted across videos

**FTC Compliance Requirement (NON-NEGOTIABLE):**
Every video with affiliate links MUST include in the YouTube description (above "Show more" fold):
```
Some links below are affiliate links. If you buy through them, I may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.
```

| Score | What It Looks/Sounds Like |
|-------|--------------------------|
| **5** | VO is crisp (-14 LUFS), music ducks properly, visuals match the color grade, film grain is consistent, transitions are intentional, captions are timed and branded. No artifacts. |
| **4** | Minor technical issues (slight audio level inconsistency, one visual slightly off-grade) that don't break immersion. |
| **3** | Acceptable quality but generic. No film grain, no vignette, flat color grade. VO is clear but music levels compete. Captions are present but unstyled. |
| **2** | Visible quality issues. Audio clipping, visual artifacts, wrong aspect ratio, unreadable text, jarring transitions. |
| **1** | Broken. Audio/video desync, missing captions on shorts, wrong fonts, bright/saturated color grade, amateur-hour quality. |

**Production Red Lines (instant score of 1):**
- Audio/video desync
- Wrong fonts (Inter Bold, Bebas Neue, or any non-brand font)
- Amber (#D4A03C) or any off-palette color in overlays
- No captions on shorts content
- Human faces visible in any generated imagery
- VO using the wrong voice or obvious AI artifacts (glitching, mispronunciation)

## 6. PRE-PUBLISH CHECKLIST

Every piece of content must pass these checks before publishing. This is the minimum bar, not the aspiration.

### For ALL Content
- [ ] No human faces visible in any frame or image
- [ ] Brand palette only (no Amber, no neon, no saturated colors)
- [ ] Correct fonts (Bangers / Montserrat / Anton ONLY)
- [ ] Red Nose character markers correct (if present): scar, bowtie, red nose, amber eyes, natural ears
- [ ] No buzzwords, hype language, or YouTuber energy in any text

### For Long-Form Videos
- [ ] Hook in first 30 seconds (no greeting, no intro)
- [ ] 6-section structure followed (Hook, Context, List, Deeper Layer, The Move, Close)
- [ ] VO at 110-125 WPM, pauses rendered correctly
- [ ] Music ducks under VO, swells during pauses
- [ ] Color grade applied (contrast, desaturation, grain, vignette)
- [ ] End screen designed (15 seconds, branded)
- [ ] FTC disclosure in description (above the fold)
- [ ] Chapters with keyword-rich titles (minimum 3)
- [ ] Tags: 2-3 branded + 5-10 topic-specific

### For Shorts/Reels
- [ ] Captions present and branded (Bangers font, Bone White, Rust Orange highlight)
- [ ] Under 59 seconds (YouTube Shorts requirement)
- [ ] Hook text in first 3 seconds
- [ ] 9:16 aspect ratio, Red Nose not cropped
- [ ] CTA present ("Full breakdown on the channel" or variant)
- [ ] #Shorts in title or description

### For Thumbnails
- [ ] 1280x720px, PNG, under 2MB
- [ ] Red Nose present and recognizable at 168x94px (mobile render size)
- [ ] Text legible at 168x94px (3-5 words, 72-96px on canvas)
- [ ] ONE accent word in Rust Orange
- [ ] Vignette (30-50% edge darkening)
- [ ] Film grain (10-15%)

### For Affiliate Content
- [ ] FTC disclosure in YouTube description (above fold)
- [ ] Product mentioned naturally (under 15 seconds in video)
- [ ] Product is 4+ stars, 500+ reviews, Prime eligible
- [ ] No hard sell, no discount codes, no "you NEED this"
- [ ] Tyrone's voice maintained throughout pitch

## 7. BRAND IDENTITY QUICK REFERENCE

### Who We Are
- A calm expert who has seen battle in the urban ghetto
- Measured. Scarred. Unshaken.
- We speak because we've lived it

### Who We Are NOT
- A conspiracy channel
- A tech hype channel
- A survivalist/prepper channel (adjacent, not core)
- A motivational channel
- An academic/lecture channel

We show you what AI is already doing in your neighborhood -- not to scare you, but to make you pay attention. And then we give you the move.

### Red Nose's Role
Red Nose is not decoration. He is Tyrone's witness. He reacts to the content. When Tyrone stops talking, Red Nose sits still. That's the punctuation.

### The Aesthetic in Three Words
Earned. Weathered. Still.

## 8. AGENT COMPLIANCE PROTOCOL

Every agent producing BowTie Bullies content MUST:

1. **Read this matrix before starting work**
2. **Score their output** using the Quick Scorecard (Section 5)
3. **Flag any Red Line violations** immediately -- do not ship
4. **Use the Pre-Publish Checklist** (Section 6) as final QA
5. **Log any brand decisions** not covered by this matrix for future governance updates

This matrix is a living document. As gaps are closed and the brand matures, dimensions will be refined and new red lines may be added. The core identity -- Tyrone's voice, Red Nose's presence, Truth Tones, and earned stillness -- is permanent.
