## The Pivot

BowTie Bullies is a **survival/preparedness channel with AI as the backdrop/catalyst.** AI explains WHY survival matters now. But the VALUE delivered is survival knowledge, not AI analysis. Tyrone saw the hood collapse before. Now AI makes it hit everyone.

AI is the accelerant — it threads through every pillar as the force multiplying economic instability, food insecurity, infrastructure failure, and institutional erosion. But AI is never the primary subject. The primary subject is: **how do you survive what's coming.**

## AI Threading Rules

AI is the BACKDROP, never the SUBJECT. For every episode:

1. **The hook can reference AI** as the reason this topic is urgent NOW
2. **The body delivers survival knowledge** — financial, food, shelter
3. **AI appears as accelerant** in the "Deeper Layer" section — explaining why historical patterns are moving faster
4. **The close is about the viewer's action** — not about AI

**Template:**
```
"AI didn't create this problem. [Economic inequality / food deserts / housing insecurity]
existed before anyone wrote a line of code. But AI made it faster. Made it invisible.
Made it harder to fight. So let's talk about what you actually do about it."
```
