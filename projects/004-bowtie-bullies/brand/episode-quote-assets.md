- Theme: Return, persistence, staying true while evolving
- Key concept: "Still taking my time to perfect the beat"
- Written by Jay-Z in 30 minutes — street awareness meets business acumen
- **Maps to:** EP 09 Still D.R.E. — economic displacement continues but so do we

### Forgot About Dre (2001, 1999)
- Theme: Legacy, being overlooked, reminding people who built the foundation
- Key concept: Listing every artist he brought up — N.W.A., Snoop, Eminem
- Response to Death Row diss tracks — turning criticism into fuel
- **Maps to:** EP 07 Forgot About Dre — the financial education they "forgot" to give us

### The Watcher (2001, 1999)
- Theme: Observation, seeing everything, strategic patience
- Key concept: Watching from above, understanding the game without getting caught in it
- **Maps to:** EP 03 The Watcher — algorithmic surveillance, housing, who controls your space

### What's the Difference (2001, 1999)
- Theme: Loyalty vs betrayal, real vs fake, industry politics
- Key concept: "Same people that tried to black ball me" — recognizing who's real
- Features Eminem and Xzibit — each addressing what separates them
- **Maps to:** EP 10 What's the Difference — financial literacy, knowing the real from the fake

### The Next Episode (2001, 1999)
- Theme: What comes next, continuation, the sequel
- **Maps to:** EP 11 The Next Episode — when the grid goes down, what's YOUR next episode

### Xxplosive (2001, 1999)
- Theme: Explosive impact, dominance, destructive force
- **Maps to:** EP 05 Xxplosive — economic detonation, financial blast radius

### Deep Water (Compton, 2015)
- Theme: Dangerous territory, survival in treacherous conditions
- Features Kendrick Lamar and Justus
- **Maps to:** EP 13 Deep Water — water security, municipal vulnerability

### I Need a Doctor (feat. Eminem & Skylar Grey)
- Theme: Needing help, recovery, coming back from the edge
- **Maps to:** EP 19 I Need a Doctor — health self-sufficiency

### Let Me Ride (The Chronic, 1992)
- Theme: Independence, moving freely, California identity
- **Maps to:** LET ME RIDE short — transportation independence

### Express Yourself (Straight Outta Compton, 1988)
- Theme: Authenticity, doing it your way, no compromise
- Dr. Dre solo performance — no profanity, pure expression
- **Maps to:** NO PROGRAM LIKE ME short — what humans have that machines don't
