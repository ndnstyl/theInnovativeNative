### Consistency Testing Protocol

1. Generate each pose 3 times
2. Compare coat color, nose color, eye color, ear shape, scar position, coveralls style across all generations
3. Select the most consistent generation as the reference for that pose
4. Store reference images in `cinema_knowledge/bowtie-bullies/poses/`
5. Use reference images for subsequent generations (image-to-image where supported)

### Vertical (9:16) Framing Rules

- Subject should never be cropped by the top 10% or bottom 14% safe zones
- Leave breathing room in the lower third for caption overlays
- The vertical environment should feel like a natural crop, not a forced reframe
- Urban vertical elements (buildings, poles, fire escapes, doorways) work with the orientation
- Avoid placing key details in the bottom 20% where platform UI obscures them
