## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Mirror EP01 visuals** — same rooftop, same Red Nose poses, but the lighting has shifted from sodium vapor night to warm amber dawn. The visual says: something changed.
- **The 10-point checklist** is the centerpiece. It makes the abstract (22 episodes of knowledge) concrete (did you DO something?). The pacing should be slow and deliberate. Each question is a moment of self-reflection for the viewer.
- **Dedication segment** carries the emotional weight of the original EP22 "Dedication" concept — dedicated to the people paying attention. This is the heart of the episode.
- **"The dog sees you."** — This is the line that lands. Red Nose has been the loyal companion all season. The audience knows what he represents. Having him "see" the viewer is the emotional payoff.
- **Close mirrors EP01** — same rooftop, same silhouette, but Red Nose walks INTO the light instead of sitting in darkness. The season's arc is visual.
