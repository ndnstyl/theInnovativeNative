### Content Pillar: Financial — 1C Wealth Building (Channel Thesis)

---

## EPISODE OVERVIEW

**Duration:** 15-20 minutes
**Emotional Arc:** Quiet/heavy (Act 1) → Grounded/real (2A) → Reflective/turning (2B) → Inspired/calm (2C) → Historical weight (3A) → Empowering/direct (3B)
**Purpose:** Establish WHO Tyrone is before establishing WHAT the channel teaches. The origin story cold open grounds the audience in lived experience. The anti-hero arc builds trust. The thesis — Each One Teach One — becomes the channel's foundational promise.

---

## PRODUCTION RULES

- **AAVE ~65%**: g-dropping, contractions, demonstrative shift, verb agreement shifts. Code-switch on technical terms.
- **Pauses are load-bearing**: [1.5s] and [2s] marked explicitly — do not compress.
- **Volume drops = emphasis** (not rises). Tyrone gets quieter when it matters most.
- **No face shown.** Hands only. Red Nose is the visual presence.
- **Nipsey: spirit, not name.** Quotes adapted to Tyrone's voice, never attributed.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Act 1 is verbatim** from `tyrone-origin-story-90s-AAVE.md`. Do not modify.
- **Volume drops are critical** — marked with *(volume drops)* and *(near whisper)*. These are the moments that land hardest.
- **No face shown. Ever.** Hands. Red Nose. City. Text. That's the visual vocabulary.
- **The stolen laptop** is the throughline object. It appears in Act 1 (cold open), Act 2A (context), Act 2B (transition), and Act 3B (barbershop reference). It's the artifact.
- **Red Nose poses** track the emotional arc:
  - Act 1: P5 (witness), P3 (endure), P1 (watch), P6 (aftermath), P4 (reflect)
  - Act 2A: P1 (observe), P2 (guard), P6 (alone)
  - Act 2B: P5 (direct address), P6 (forward movement), P1 (loyalty)
  - Act 2C: P2 (strength), P1 (patience)
  - Act 3A: P2 (grounded)
  - Act 3B: P1 (watch → warm), P4 (watchful protector)

- **"Each One Teach One"** appears exactly 3 times: once when introduced (3A), once after the history (3A close), once in the close (3B). Not overused. Each instance carries weight.
