# Iconic Brand Buildings — Reimagined

## Guidelines for Adding New Brands

1. **Recognizability** — The brand's logo or signature visual must be identifiable from a drone viewpoint
2. **Architectural plausibility** — The building must look like it could actually be constructed
3. **Physics-respecting** — Real materials, real structural engineering, real construction sequences
4. **Hyper-realism** — Photorealistic rendering, not cartoonish or stylized
5. **Construction narrative** — The 8-phase timelapse must make sense across 8 keyframes showing progressive construction from raw land through to a fully activated building
