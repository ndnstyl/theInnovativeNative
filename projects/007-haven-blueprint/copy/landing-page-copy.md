Every piece of content — from initial concept to published video — lives in one Airtable base with 9 linked tables. This is your pipeline's nervous system. You will always know what stage every piece of content is in.

n8n workflows chain everything together. A single trigger fires the script generator, which feeds the image generator scene by scene, which feeds the video assembler. Status updates land in Airtable automatically at every step.

**What you get:**
- Master orchestrator workflow (chains all sub-workflows in sequence)
- Webhook integration connecting Airtable status changes to n8n triggers
- Error handling patterns with retry logic, fallback flows, and failure notifications
- n8n setup guides for both self-hosted (Docker) and cloud deployments

Gemini AI generates images from structured scene prompts. A built-in quality scoring system rejects anything below a 7/10 threshold and auto-regenerates up to 3 times. For video, Veo3.1 and Sora2 generation through Kie.AI gives you motion content at scale.

**What you get:**

- Asset generator workflow (scene prompt > Gemini image > quality scoring > Drive upload)
- UGC Machine workflow template for Veo3.1 and Sora2 dual-model video generation
- Quality scoring rubric with the 7/10 threshold system

### Step 5: Assemble & Publish

FFMPEG takes your generated scene images, applies Ken Burns zoom effects, crossfade transitions, branded text overlays, and background music — then outputs a platform-ready MP4. One command. Done.

**What you get:**
- FFMPEG assembler workflow (images + music > Ken Burns + crossfade + overlays > MP4)
- Command recipe library for every effect (zoom, crossfade, text overlays, music mixing)
- Platform export presets for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts
- Output spec reference (1080x1920, H.264, CRF 18, AAC 128kbps, 30fps)
