# LinkedIn Platform Specifications

## Overview

LinkedIn is a professional B2B network. Best for targeting by job title, company, industry, and seniority. Higher CPCs but higher-quality leads for B2B.

## Best Practices

### Content Strategy

1. **Post Timing**
   - Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
   - Best times: 7-8am, 12pm, 5-6pm (local time)
   - Avoid weekends (40% lower engagement)

2. **Post Frequency**
   - Optimal: 2-5 posts per week
   - Pages: 1 post per day max
   - Personal: 1-2 posts per day max

3. **Content Mix**
   - 80% value-driven content
   - 20% promotional content
   - Include native video (5x more engagement)
   - Use documents/carousels for how-to content

### Ad Best Practices

1. **Targeting**
   - Start with job titles + seniority
   - Layer in company size and industry
   - Use Matched Audiences for retargeting
   - Minimum audience: 50,000+ for learning

2. **Creative**
   - Use faces in images (real people)
   - Contrast colors stand out in feed
   - Clear, readable text (mobile first)
   - Test square vs landscape

3. **Copy**
   - Lead with benefit, not feature
   - Ask questions to drive engagement
   - Use numbers and statistics
   - Include clear CTA

### Engagement Tactics

1. **Hook in first line** - First 140 chars are critical
2. **Use line breaks** - White space increases readability
3. **Tag relevant people** - Increases reach
4. **Reply to comments** - Within first hour especially
5. **Use native features** - Polls, documents, newsletters

## Compliance Notes

1. **Ad Policies**
   - No misleading claims
   - No discriminatory targeting
   - Professional tone required
   - Clear advertiser identification

2. **Lead Gen Forms**
   - Privacy policy required
   - Consent language required
   - Data handling compliance (GDPR, CCPA)

3. **Restricted Categories**
   - Financial services (extra requirements)
   - Healthcare (region-specific rules)
   - Employment (fair housing compliance)
