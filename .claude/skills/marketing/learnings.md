## Facebook Ads - Sabri Suby (2026 Tutorial)

**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ea1hFxPx3JA
**Date Extracted:** 2026-02-05

### Core Philosophy

1. **Don't obsess over algorithms** - Focus on human psychology, not media buying tactics
2. **Compete for attention** - You're competing with Netflix, TMZ, UFC, not your direct competitors
3. **Dopamine is the currency** - Your ad must flood the prospect's brain with dopamine

### The Hyper-Dopamine Ad Formula

```
Clickbait + Targeted Benefit = Hyper-Dopamine Ad
```

Three components:
1. **Pattern Interrupt** - Scroll-stopping image
2. **Burning Intrigue** - Curiosity that demands a click
3. **Big Specific Benefit** - Clear value proposition for your target market

### Market Awareness Pyramid

| Segment | % | Description |
|---------|---|-------------|
| Ready to Buy | 3% | Looking to purchase now |
| Info Gathering | 17% | Researching options |
| Problem Aware | 20% | Know they have a problem |
| Not Aware | 60% | Don't know they have a problem |

**Key Insight:** Facebook lets you target the ENTIRE pyramid because you're pushing messages to people whether they're thinking about it or not.

### What Drives Clicks

Ranked by power:
1. **Curiosity** (most powerful)
2. Intrigue
3. Shock
4. Direct benefit
5. Implied benefit
6. Fear
7. Vanity
8. Self-interest

### The Two Jobs of Every Ad

1. **Deliver news, value, or findings** - What people actually want
2. **Sell the click** - Create irresistible curiosity

### Ad Anatomy Checklist

- [ ] Scroll-stopping image (pattern interrupt)
- [ ] Eyeball-grabbing headline
- [ ] Slippery lead-in copy (most overlooked!)
- [ ] Butter body copy (max 2200 chars for FB+IG)
- [ ] Intriguing link description
- [ ] Non-threatening CTA ("Learn More" wins)

### Creative Styles That Work

1. **Raw Native** - iPhone photos, looks like user content
2. **SMS Ads** - Text message format
3. **Breaking News** - News headline style (Sabri's #1)
4. **Native Highlight** - Red circles, arrows
5. **Native Social Post** - Lad Bible format
6. **Secret Info** - Maps, revealed info

### Headline Swipe Formulas

| Original (Gossip) | Adapted (Business) |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| "Taylor Swift fans going wild after spotting X-rated detail" | "Aussies going wild after spotting home loan hack" |
| "iPhone user advised to do one thing to stop battery draining" | "CEOs advised to do one thing before going home to family" |
| "Insane new documentary leaves viewers screaming" | "Insane new mindset trick leaves anxiety sufferers speechless" |
| "Man buys mystery reptile egg to hatch" | "Miami mom discovers mystery metabolism booster" |
| "Secret way to find out who called you without Google" | "Secret way to find $5M business funding without banks" |

### Copy Rules

1. **Write at 5th grade level or below** (use Hemingway App)
2. **Short words, sentences, paragraphs**
3. **Lots of line breaks** (mobile readability)
4. **Write "you" not "our customers"**
5. **Write "we/us" not your brand name**
6. **Add specificity** - "12-19 kilos" not "lose weight"
7. **Don't look like an ad**
8. **Sell the click, not the product**

### Positive vs Negative Hooks

**8 out of 10 times, positive hooks outperform negative ones.**

- Bad: "Don't put on 50 kilos this winter, do this one thing"
- Good: "Lose 20 to 50 kgs with this one weird hack"

### CTA Testing Results

**"Learn More" wins.** Don't use Buy Now, Inquire Now, etc.

## Meta: Transcription Process Learnings

### Technical

1. **yt-dlp with Chrome cookies** bypasses YouTube bot detection
2. **VTT files have massive redundancy** - need Python cleaning script
3. **Group by minute** produces readable ~35 blocks for 35-min video
4. **Character limit awareness** - some cleaned transcripts still exceed 25K tokens

### Content Extraction

1. **Visual timestamps guide** is essential for screenshot capture
2. **Framework extraction** is highest-value output
3. **Headline swipe files** are immediately actionable
4. **Checklist format** makes frameworks easy to apply
