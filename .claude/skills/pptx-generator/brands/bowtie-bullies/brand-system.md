# BowTie Bullies — The Aftermath | Brand System

> Raw. Earned. Restrained. A faceless AI channel narrated by Tyrone, witnessed by Red Nose — an American Red Nose Pitbull in sleeveless black coveralls and tactical boots, who chose composure over chaos.

---

## Brand Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Earned Authority**
   Content comes from lived experience navigating broken systems — policing, housing, education, economic exclusion. AI is just the next system that wasn't built for us. No hype, no panic. Just pattern recognition from someone who's been here before.

2. **Restrained Power**
   Every word, every frame, every pause earns its place. No neon, no saturation, no shouting. The coveralls on the pitbull say it all — strength that chose restraint. The BowTie Mark on vehicles and in the environment ties the world together.

3. **Truth Tones**
   Dark Steel, Rust Orange, Earth Clay, Ash Gray, Bone White. The palette of a world that's already fallen. Warm enough to feel human. Cold enough to feel real.

4. **The Mascot IS the Brand**
   Red Nose — a 2D cel-shaded American Red Nose Pitbull in sleeveless black coveralls and tactical boots — appears in every thumbnail, every intro, every outro. He's the visual anchor. Tyrone is the voice. Together they are the channel.

### Color Rules
1. **Dark Steel is the default.** Everything lives on this.
2. **Rust Orange is your one accent.** Use it like you use your voice — sparingly, with weight.
3. **Bone White is text.** Clean against dark. Never pure `#FFFFFF`.
4. **No gradients** unless they're vignette shadows.
5. **No bright colors.** If it would glow on a screen, it doesn't belong.
6. **Earth Clay and Ash Gray** fill the space between — structural, not decorative.

### Size Hierarchy (Video Overlays)

| Element | Size | Font | Color |

| Keyword Accent | Same as title | Anton | Rust Orange `#9A4C22` |

| Data / Stats | 36-48px | JetBrains Mono | Rust Orange `#9A4C22` |
| Source Citation | 20-24px | Space Mono | Ash Gray `#4A4A4A` |

### Typography Rules
1. Headlines = UPPERCASE. Always. No exceptions.
2. Body text = sentence case. Monospaced. Feels like typed notes.
3. Data = monospaced. Numbers, stats, dates. Like instrument readings.
4. Never decorative fonts. No scripts, no serifs.
5. Letter-spacing: +2-4% on headlines (intentional breathing room).

## Voice Summary (Tyrone)

**In one sentence:** A calm expert who has seen battle in the African American urban ghetto. Measured. Scarred. Unshaken.

### Name Protocol
His crew calls him **"T"**, **"Unk"**, or **"OG"** — never his actual name. If someone slips and says "Tyrone," they get the deadliest stare-down. No words. Just eyes. It doesn't happen twice.

### Four Pillars
1. **Emotional, Not Exposed** — Subtext over confession. Pain held steady, not displayed.
2. **Stoic, Not Cold** — Short to medium sentences. Clean breaks. Silence talks.
3. **Confident, Not Loud** — Declarative statements. No hype. No excess punctuation.
4. **Raw, Not Chaotic** — Plain language. Minimal polish. Natural rhythm. Every word earns its place.

### AI/Tech Voice Adaptations
- Connects AI to systems he's already survived
- Uses street-level analogies for technical concepts
- Frames AI impact through lived experience
- "Predictive policing" → "profiling with a computer"
- "Algorithmic bias" → "The computer learned who to ignore"

### Tone Spectrum
Conversational. Serious. Bold. Emotional. Raw. Never dramatic. Never hyped.

## Red Nose — Mascot Guidelines

### Physical Description (Canonical)
```
Breed:      American Red Nose Pitbull (APBT)
Coat:       Rich reddish-brown / copper, short, smooth, muscular definition visible
Nose:       Distinctive red/liver nose (NOT black)
Eyes:       Amber/honey colored, intelligent, calm, alert
Build:      Strong, muscular, realistic — NOT exaggerated bodybuilder
Ears:       Natural (uncropped), rose-shaped, folded back
Expression: Controlled intelligence, gentleman strategist — not aggressive, not beast mode
Age:        Adult, prime (3-5 years equivalent)
Scar:       Slight battle scar across left brow (earned, healed)
```

### Wardrobe
```
Coveralls:  Sleeveless black coveralls, slightly unzipped over bare muscular chest
Boots:      Black military tactical boots (urban operator)
Gloves:     Optional — black tactical gloves (when context suits)
```

Red Nose does NOT wear the BowTie Mark on his body. The Chevrolet bowtie emblem appears only on vehicles and as environmental easter eggs.

### The BowTie Mark (Brand Symbol — NOT on character)
```
Shape:     Chevrolet bowtie silhouette (horizontal diamond-cross)
Fill:      Matte black (#1E1E20)
Trim:      Ultra-thin deep crimson red (#8B0000) — brake-light red
Use:       Vehicles, watermarks, environmental easter eggs ONLY
Rule:      ≤5% of visual frame. Never on Red Nose's body.
```

### Pose Library (6 Core Poses)

| ID | Name | Description | Use |
|----|------|-------------|-----|
| P1 | The Watch | Sitting upright, 3/4 angle, looking off-frame | Primary thumbnail, banner |
| P2 | The Guard | Standing, facing camera, low angle | Authority thumbnails |
| P3 | The Companion | Lying beside object, relaxed but awake | Product/affiliate content |
| P4 | The Silhouette | Back to camera, rooftop edge, city below | Philosophical content |
| P5 | The Close | Extreme close-up, one eye + scar, shallow DOF | Hooks, shorts, drama |
| P6 | The Aftermath | Walking through urban ruins, mid-stride | Series identity, merch |

### Consistency Markers (MUST include in every AI prompt)
```
"American Red Nose Pitbull, adult male, strong muscular realistic build,
rich reddish-brown copper coat, distinctive red/liver nose,
amber/honey eyes, natural uncropped rose ears, small healed scar
across left brow, wearing sleeveless black coveralls slightly unzipped
over bare muscular chest, black military tactical boots.
Controlled intelligence, gentleman strategist expression. NOT aggressive.
NOT playful. Watchful. Calm. 2D cel-shaded style."
```

### Negative Prompt (ALWAYS include)
```

aggressive snarling, teeth showing, cute puppy, playful, tongue out,
bright colors, butterfly bowtie, fabric bowtie, silk bowtie,
bowtie at collar, exaggerated bodybuilder, beast mode,
suburban setting, grass yard, dog park, stock photo, cheerful,
multiple dogs, human face, neon, photorealistic, 3D render"
```

```

Film Grain: 10-15%
Shadow:     4px 4px 16px rgba(0,0,0,0.9) on text
Export:     PNG, <2MB
```

## FFMPEG Post-Processing

### Color Grade Filter
```bash
-vf "eq=contrast=1.15:brightness=-0.03:saturation=0.75,
     unsharp=5:5:0.5,noise=alls=20:allf=t+u,vignette=PI/4:1.2"
```

### Audio Mix Levels
```
VO:          -12 to -14 dB (primary)
Music:       -26 to -32 dB (felt, not heard)
Silence:     -inf (intentional 1.5-3 second gaps, music dips to -40dB)
Ambient SFX: -22 to -28 dB (sparse)
```
