## Platform Learnings

### Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)

#### Creative Learnings

1. **Hyper-Dopamine Ads Outperform**
   - Pattern interrupt images (unusual, eye-catching)
   - Dual images (primary + secondary) for intrigue
   - Native, non-ad look performs best
   - Source: Sabri Suby Tutorial

2. **Headline Formulas That Work**
   - Swipe from gossip sites (Lad Bible, TMZ, E-News)
   - "[Group] going wild after spotting [discovery]"
   - "[Role] advised to do one thing to [benefit]"
   - "Insane new [thing] leaves [audience] speechless"
   - "Secret way to [achieve goal] without [obstacle]"

3. **Copy Rules**
   - 5th grade reading level (Hemingway App)
   - Short sentences, lots of line breaks
   - 125 chars visible before "See More"
   - Sell the click, not the product

#### Targeting Learnings

1. **Start Broad**
   - Advantage+ audiences often outperform detailed targeting
   - Let algorithm optimize with 50+ conversions/week
   - Source: Meta best practices

2. **Lookalike Sizing**
   - 1% for highest quality
   - 1-3% for balanced approach
   - 3%+ for scale (lower quality)

#### Optimization Learnings

1. **Campaign Config Documentation Pattern (2026-02-05)**
   - Create comprehensive ad set configs that reference (not duplicate) related configs
   - Include: audience mapping, creative mapping, budget allocation, benchmarks, optimization rules, launch checklist
   - Cross-reference T006 (creative), T007 (copy), T009 (retargeting), T010 (lookalike) for modularity
   - Always include troubleshooting guide for common issues (ad rejection, low delivery, high CPA)

2. **Budget Allocation Best Practice**
   - Retargeting: 50% (highest intent, warmest audience)
   - Lookalike: 40% (prospecting, broader reach)
   - Lead Gen: 10% (direct capture, test phase)

3. **Automated Rules Setup**
   - CPA cap rule: Pause if CPL > $150 AND impressions > 1,000
   - Scale rule: +20% budget if CPL < $75 AND conversions > 3
   - Frequency alert: Notify at 4.5 (refresh creative before fatigue)

## Bidding & Budget Learnings

1. **Learning Phase**
   - Don't touch ads for 7 days after launch
   - Need 50+ conversions/week for stable optimization
   - Source: Meta, Google documentation

2. **Budget Scaling**
   - Increase by max 20% every 3-4 days
   - Sudden increases reset learning
   - Source: Industry best practice

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Don't edit during learning phase** - Resets optimization
2. **Don't obsess over algorithms** - Focus on creative and psychology
3. **Don't use blind clickbait** - Must have targeted benefit
4. **Don't forget mobile** - 80%+ of users are on mobile
