## Strategic Learnings

### Campaign Strategy

1. **Positive hooks outperform negative 8/10 times**
   - "Lose 20 kilos" beats "Don't gain 50 kilos"
   - Lead with benefit, not fear
   - Source: Sabri Suby FB Ads Tutorial

2. **Hyper-Dopamine Ad Formula**
   - Pattern Interrupt + Burning Intrigue + Big Specific Benefit
   - Compete with Netflix/TMZ, not competitors
   - Source: Sabri Suby

3. **Market Awareness Pyramid**
   - 3% ready to buy (Google Search)
   - 17% information gathering
   - 20% problem aware
   - 60% not problem aware
   - Facebook targets entire pyramid

## Competitive Research Validation Learnings

### Pain Point Prioritization Framework - 2026-02-06
**Context**: Validating Maya's 42 pain points from competitive research
**Discovery**: Three-factor scoring (Emotional Resonance + Differentiation Clarity + Ad Copy Potential) effectively prioritizes pain points for campaign use
**Action**: Use E+D+A scoring (1-5 each, max 15) for future pain point ranking
**Source**: Week 1 Cerebro competitive research validation

### Lock-in Dominates Legal AI Pain - 2026-02-06
**Context**: Analyzing cross-competitor themes
**Discovery**: Contract lock-in and pricing concerns outweigh feature complaints 2:1. Lawyers hate feeling trapped more than they hate missing features.
**Action**: Lead with "freedom" messaging (month-to-month, cancel anytime) before features
**Source**: Harvey/Lexis/CoCounsel research showing 20+ lock-in mentions vs 8 feature mentions

### The 17% Statistic is Gold - 2026-02-06
**Context**: Reviewing Lexis+ accuracy concerns
**Discovery**: Stanford study finding 17% hallucination rate for Lexis+ AI is highly credible and emotionally resonant. Specific + authoritative source = powerful.
**Action**: Prominently feature this stat in accuracy-focused campaigns. "17% wrong" is scarier than "sometimes wrong."
**Source**: Reddit r/Lawyertalk citing Stanford study

### Mid-Market Whitespace is Real - 2026-02-06
**Context**: Analyzing competitive positioning gaps
**Discovery**: All three competitors explicitly or implicitly target enterprise/BigLaw. Harvey quote "only viable for the very top of the market" confirms the gap.
**Action**: Own "50-500 attorney firms" positioning. This is defensible territory competitors have abandoned.
**Source**: Harvey eesel.ai review, general pricing analysis

### Practice-Specific Research Needs More Work - 2026-02-06
**Context**: Assessing bankruptcy and federal criminal segment data
**Discovery**: General mid-market data is strong. Practice-specific (bankruptcy, federal criminal) data is thin - only inference from general pain points.
**Action**: Run general mid-market campaign first while gathering practice-specific testimonials and research. Don't stretch thin data.
**Source**: Week 1 research gap analysis
