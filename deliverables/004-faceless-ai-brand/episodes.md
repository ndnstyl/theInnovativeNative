## How This File Works

Every episode title is drawn from Nipsey Hussle or Dr. Dre's catalog — used literally, flipped, or remixed to fit the BowTie Bullies world. The audience that knows the reference feels it deeper. The audience that doesn't still gets a title that hits.

**Content Matrix:** 60% Financial Survival / 30% Food, Resources, Shelter / 10% When The Center Doesn't Hold
**AI Threading:** AI is the backdrop and accelerant in every episode — never the primary subject. The value delivered is survival knowledge.

**Format per entry:**
- **Source** → Original song/album
- **Episode Title** → BowTie Bullies adaptation
- **Theme** → What this episode is about (survival-focused)
- **Content Pillar** → Financial (1A/1B/1C) / Food-Shelter (2A/2B/2C) / Center Doesn't Hold (3)
- **Thumbnail Type** → Which Red Nose pose (P1-P6)
- **Affiliate Angle** → Natural product tie-in (if any)
