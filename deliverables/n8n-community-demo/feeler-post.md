Hey all -- been using n8n for about a year now and have 49 workflows running on my own server. Not selling anything. Just genuinely curious if anyone here would want to see how some of these actually work under the hood.

I was thinking about doing a quick screenshare -- maybe 15 minutes walking through three workflows at different levels, then 10 minutes for questions. Not a course. Not a webinar. Just showing what I built and how it runs.

Here's what I'd walk through:


**Simple (3 nodes)**

A time tracking logger. Webhook fires, maps the fields, writes to Airtable. Set it up once, never touch it again. Takes maybe 5 minutes to build.


**Intermediate**

A social media workflow that runs on a schedule. AI writes the post, AI generates the image, publishes to LinkedIn and Instagram, then pings me on Slack. I just review and approve. The whole thing runs without me.


**Advanced**

This one's my favorite. I call it Trend Pilot. You point it at any channel or hashtag on basically any platform. It scrapes the top-performing posts, filters by real engagement -- we're talking 1K+ likes or 10K+ views -- then AI reads through the comments for sentiment and builds a full playbook of what's working and why.

I can show the actual Airtable results for one specific channel and the playbook it generated. Real data, not a slide deck.


Quick note on setup: I self-host on a $5/month server with unlimited workflows. But there's also a free cloud tier if you just want to try it out.

Again -- not pitching anything. No product, no offer, no "link in bio." I just think this stuff is genuinely useful and most people don't know it exists.

If you'd want to watch something like that, drop a "yes" in the comments or let me know what you'd actually want to see.

No pressure either way.
